Our goods

  • Baltic amber is the most popular in the world, also named as the Gold of the North. Baltic amber is less hard and should be processed very carefully.

    Baltic Raw Amber

  • Ukrainian amber is the second most popular in the world, known as the Sun Stone. It is very dense, so easily polished, drilled, and good for the production of high-quality jewelry.

    Ukrainian Raw Amber

  • Dominican amber can be found in many colors, besides the obvious amber. Yellow and honey colored are fairly common. There is also red and green in smaller quantities and the rare blue amber. Only experienced professionals should grind and polish this amber, because of its high price and softness.

    Dominican Raw Amber
